ROAD SAFETY DOWN UNDER: A 5-Year Analysis of Fatalities on Australian Roads

NAME: THANH TRUNG TRAN

The australian road deaths Datbase (ARDD) provide basic detail of road transport crash fatalities in Australia as reported by the police each month to the State and Territory road safety authorities. This website aims to generate several visualisations to delve into and perform a more insightful analysis on the number of crashes and fatalities in different States of Australia in the last 5 years - from Jan 2018 to July 2023
